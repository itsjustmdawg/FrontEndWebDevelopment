About Me

My Values

Tolerant

I deem myself to be a tolerant individual. I will usually accept a person's apology if they had wronged me in any way. Personally, I believe that everyone deserves a second chance to prove to themselves & others that they have changed for the better.

Courageous

Never once in my life have I ever backed down from reaching my goals due to fear. I will move forward until I reach my goals.

Diligent

As a student, my diligence plays the most important role in determining my success as a student. Nothing comes easy in this world, dicipline and diligence are required to master a skill.

My Interests

Cooking

I have always been interested in cooking. I learned how to cook by observing my mother when she cooks in the kitchen. I cooked my first dish when I was 14 years old which was Grilled Salmon. I see cooking as a way of creating art and making people happy. I prefer cooking foods from the Western and Mediterranean diet.

Coding

Staying at home for 2 years during the recent Covid-19 pandemic period has allowed me to develop an interest in coding. I enrolled for Harvard's free CS50 course on edX.org and learned how to code in C. I then moved on to teach myself Python and Javascript. I realised that I had a flair for coding as I could grasp certain programming concepts very quickly. I also find coding to be very stimulating and I could sit and code for hours.
